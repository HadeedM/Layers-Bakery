About Us

Founded in 2020 in the heart of Lahore, Layers Bakeshop was born from a simple passion — crafting extraordinary cakes that tell a story with every slice.

We specialize in premium, handcrafted layered cakes made with the finest Belgian chocolate, fresh cream cheese, and rich mousse fillings. From our iconic Lotus Biscoff to our indulgent Belgian Malt, every cake is a masterpiece.

Our mission is to bring joy to every celebration — birthdays, weddings, Eid, and everyday indulgences.
